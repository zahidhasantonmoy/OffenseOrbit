About OffenseOrbit

Building trust and efficiency in crime management for a safer Bangladesh

What is OffenseOrbit?

OffenseOrbit is an advanced crime management platform designed to make communication between citizens and law enforcement seamless. By harnessing technology, OffenseOrbit helps streamline processes and ensure public safety.

How OffenseOrbit Works

Key Features

  • Citizen-Centric Features: Citizens can report crimes, search for case statuses, and provide feedback through the platform.
  • Law Enforcement Tools: Law enforcement officers can update case details, track reports, and interact directly with citizens.
  • Safety Zone Mapping: Visualizes danger zones across three levels to prioritize public safety.
  • Knowledge Hub: Offers crime prevention tips, blogs, and safety updates for public awareness.

Our Mission

OffenseOrbit strives to empower communities by fostering collaboration between citizens and law enforcement agencies. Our goal is to create a safer environment for everyone by leveraging cutting-edge technology.

  • Establish a sense of security among citizens.
  • Provide law enforcement with actionable data to expedite resolutions.
  • Prioritize user privacy and data confidentiality.
