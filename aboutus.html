About OffenseOrbit

Building trust and efficiency in crime management for a safer Bangladesh

What is OffenseOrbit?

OffenseOrbit is a comprehensive Crime Record Management System specifically designed to enhance communication between law enforcement and the public in Bangladesh. Our platform bridges the gap between safety providers and citizens while ensuring efficient crime data management.

Key Features

  • User Interaction: Enables the public to report crimes, search case records, provide feedback, and access area safety information.
  • Police Management: Allows officers to update case statuses, manage cases, and track helpline reports efficiently.
  • Real-Time Tools: Features a live chat system for direct interaction between users and the police, police station location mapping, and danger zone visualization categorized into three levels (Dangerous, More Dangerous, Very Dangerous).
  • Information Hub: Provides a blog/news section with crime prevention tips, updates, and user comments with sharing options.
  • User-Friendly Design: A responsive layout featuring a structured top bar, side menus, and interactive sections with maps and live tools.
  • Secure Login System: Offers login/registration functionality for a personalized experience, ensuring data security and access control.

Why Choose OffenseOrbit?

Our platform is tailored for Bangladeshi law enforcement and citizens, focusing on trust, efficiency, and public safety. Here's why OffenseOrbit is the right choice:

  • Strengthened collaboration between the public and law enforcement.
  • Comprehensive mapping tools with real-time updates on crime-prone zones.
  • Instant notifications for updates on cases, safety alerts, and news.
  • A strong focus on user privacy and confidentiality.
